BRARWL is an augmented reality mobile multiplayer game about fighting and surviving. By looking on the game board through the camera on the mobile phone, the player can control the character with an on-screen gamepad. There are five different characters to choose from, each with different skills and abilities. The player can also interact with the game environment with physical cards that add digital objects to the game.

Learn more at brarwl.com
