The Feeling of Happiness and the State of Health 2010-2014

By Niclas Ericsson | Data from WVS

The size of the head depends on the state of health for the specific country. The smile on the other hand depends on the feeling of happiness. By looking at them both, one can see correlations between the two sets of data. Click on the heads to view statistics for that specific country.
